# Momo House

The single home for Monica Boccardi's Momo ecosystem.

## Experiences

- `/` — The House of Rooms
- `/editions/text/` — the complete reading edition
- `/editions/living/` — the complete living edition
- `/experiences/quiet/` — The Quiet Rooms

## Publishing rule

`main` is production. All work is prepared on a short-lived branch, checked in preview, and merged only after the prose and presentation are approved.

The complete manuscript currently contains the introductory note and Rooms I–XII. No edition may publish fewer rooms than the canonical manuscript without clearly identifying itself as an excerpt.

## Author workflow

Monica supplies writing and approves the rendered chapter. The technical maintainer handles structure, previews, checks, publishing, links, and archives.
